We had a wonderful hangout [today](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OAN7W2zVRaI), in which we talked about a lot of things. One of them was the actual size of our team and the obvious lack of time to address everything.

In an effort to reduce the workload and support, we started a few months ago, deprecating and deleting repositories that were under the Phalcon organization but were never used or maintained. Recently however, we discovered that we had close to 20 repositories hosting applications written in Phalcon. Those applications were more or less identical to one another, so in essence were not offering guidance and examples on how to create an application in Phalcon.

As mentioned in our hangout today, we are a small team and just updating all of our applications would be a lengthy task which will take time from development towards the framework. Those repositories represented some of our sites, such as this blog, the [main website](https://phalconphp.com), the [Zephir blog](https://blog.zephir-lang.com), [Zephir website](https://zephir-lang.com), the [BuiltWith](https://builtwith.phalconphp.com) site etc.
